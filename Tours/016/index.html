MONTERVERDE AVENTURE (EXTREME BUNGEE OR SUPER SWING).

Puntarenas, Costa Rica

In recent years, Monteverde has managed to combine its tradition in ecotourism and conservation with the adventure tourism, and has succeeded in ways that would put to doubt even the most intrepid.

This tour is designed especially for the stout-hearted, the fearless, the courageous, the boldâ€¦ And for sure for anyone else who wants to try to jump! In short, this is a tour for all those enjoy or want to start enjoying the adventures!

On this tour we have 3 options that will serve us for a good adventure:

Extreme Bungee

Unique in its kind since the jump is made from a Tram moving by a cable system to the point where the most daring jump into the void from a height of 143 meters, becoming the highest in Costa Rica and Central America. Our cable system was brought from Canada and our guides are certified by the ICT. You need to weigh more than 100 pounds for the tour.

Super Swing

This is a super swing through which you're secured by two harness, chest and waist plus helmet and carabiners to a rope of 50 meters which is tensioned to achieve the effect of Swing, so you will be suspended over 100 meters high!! ... Where you can not only live the experience of feeling like flying but also a glimpse of the majestic beauty of the landscape!

Extreme Canopy

The Extreme Canopy offers a modern and safe equipment, safety instructions, guides extensive experience, overlooking a waterfall, 14 cables, 5 of them extremely long: 467 meters, 425 meters, 600 meters, 750 meters, 1 kilometer.

The cables have a height between 75 meters and 150 meters approximate.

The Canopy included:

  • Rappel (30 mts).
  • Tarzan Swing.
  • Cable -Superman 1 km.
  • Superman Underground: Tunnel under the mountain of 175 meters long..
  • Total distance of 2.8 km. Total duration of the tour 3 hours..

Activities Include

  • Comfortable A/C round trip transportation with bilingual guide.
  • Extreme bungee or super swing.
  • Typical Costa Rican lunch.

Proper attire

  • Comfortable clothing.
  • Tennis shoes.
  • Change of clothes.
  • Sunblock.
  • Insect repellent.
