PARQUE NACIONAL RINCÓN DE LA VIEJA (SECTOR PAILAS).

Guanacaste, Costa Rica

Rincon de la Vieja National Park is a place where volcanic components known as fumaroles and volcanic bubbling mud lagoons are combined with a variety of ecosystems and stunning scenery.

This is a tour for those who enjoy hiking, as we’ll walk along the circular 'Pailas" trail for approximately two hours (3 km). The main attraction of the trail is the observation of volcanic manifestations (fumaroles, mud flows & fumaroles lagoon) with temperatures between 78 ° C to 106 ° C. Passing through both wooded and open areas, you will soon see the transition from wet to dry forest while getting a good chance of spotting a wide variety of birds, mammals and reptiles.

After finishing our visit to these geothermal activities, we would return to Liberia to enjoy a well-deserved lunch and try to see a little more about the “White City” and its people.

Activities Include

These tours include the following activities:

  • Comfortable A/C round trip transportation with bilingual guide.
  • Guided tour to see volcanic activity, as well as the flora and fauna of the park.
  • Costa Rican typical lunch.
  • Liberia City Tour.
  • If everyone on tour agrees, we can visit other trails in the park to enjoy more time in the woods.

Proper attire

  • Hiking boots.
  • A change of clothes.
  • Towels.
  • Rain coat.
  • Sunblock.
  • Insect repellent.
  • A hat or cap.
  • Energy bars and water.
