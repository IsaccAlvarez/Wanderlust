WHITE WATER RAFTING

River adventure. Guanacaste. Costa Rica

Located in the heart of Guanacaste, the This River will test your courage and challenge your skills in its incredible rapids. Our journey will start where the dry and rain forest blend their beauties creating a magic place surrounded by lust vegetation, house of an enormous variety of animals, where you will see some of the migratory birds, butterflies, monkies, and reptiles.

For those already rafters, this trip is a must. Its continues challenges awaits you as you plunge through twists, turns and drops down 8 miles of intense rapids. (Not runable all year) this is a pure magic trip, unsurpassed beauty, and stunning wildlife scenery that will take your breath away. Who can go? 12 years and up. No previous experience is needed. Pregnancy woman are not allowed. All participants must be in good physical conditions and ready to be active all day

Activities Include

These tours include the following activities:

  • Comfortable A/C round trip transportation with bilingual guide.
  • Professional bilingual rafting guides.
  • Rafting equipment.
  • About 2.5 h on rafting.
  • Snacks and lunch.

If we have time, we can visit the Las Pumas Rescue Center, where you can observe in captivity several species of felines as the stunning Jaguar, as well as several species of monkeys, weasels and birds. The price of admission is $ 12 USD P/P and it is not included in the tour and that money is used to finance the rescue center. It is a non-profit Foundation.

Proper attire

  • Rubber soled shoes that stay on and can get wet, tennis shoes or similar. Flip flops are no recommended.
  • Secure head strap for sunglasses.
  • A change of clothes.
  • Towels.
  • Sunblock.
  • Insect repellent.
  • A hat or cap.
  • Last minute shopping can be done at the rafting souvenir shop.
