Sport Fishing

Sport fishing is a very important tourist attraction in Guanacaste and Costa Rica in general. In Wanderlust Adventures, we work with about 25 captains in the area of Tamarindo and Flamingo and at least 20 captains in the Central and South Pacific. We have availability of different types of fishing boats . From the smallest with professional fishing equipment but without electronic tracking of fish, to medium and large boats outfitted with tournament quality gear (shimano-Penn) and state –of the art electronics that will take you where the fish are.

In most cases, our captains are local in their respective areas and some have up to 15 years of experience in sport fishing in the same part of the country, also they know their workplace so well that will take you to best fishing spots without much help from the fish finder. Those who are fishermen may agree that a high percentage of success of a fishing trip depends on knowledge of Capitan, and in this sense our captains are known to spare no effort for you to have a great day of fishing.
